Access Denied

Your email domain is not authorized to access this application.

What happened?

You attempted to sign in with: an unauthorized email

This application is restricted to users with @jkkn.ac.in email addresses.

What can you do?

  • Sign in with your JKKN institutional email (@jkkn.ac.in)
  • If you believe you should have access, contact your administrator
  • Make sure you're selecting the correct Google account

Authentication Failed

There was a problem with the Google sign-in process.

What happened?

The authentication request was cancelled or encountered an error. This can happen if:

  • You cancelled the sign-in process
  • Your browser blocked pop-ups or cookies
  • There was a network issue
  • The session timed out

Security Check Failed

The authentication request could not be verified.

What happened?

The security token for your sign-in request was invalid or expired. This is a safety measure to protect against cross-site request forgery attacks.

What can you do?

  • Try signing in again from the home page
  • Clear your browser cookies and try again
  • Make sure cookies are enabled in your browser

Something Went Wrong

An unexpected error occurred during authentication.

What can you do?

  • Try signing in again
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • If the problem persists, contact support
